Welcome to LeGros Landscapes & Construction LTD

At LeGros Landscapes, we are passionate about creating exceptional outdoor spaces that reflect the natural beauty of New Zealand. Specializing in native revegetation and residential landscaping, we bring together professionalism and a sense of fun to transform your vision into reality.

Whether you're looking to restore a native bushland, create a garden oasis, or design a functional and inviting outdoor living area, our expertise in native revegetation and residential landscaping ensures that your project is in capable hands.

We combine our deep understanding of New Zealand's native plant species with innovative design principles to craft landscapes that are not only visually stunning but also harmonious with the local ecosystem. Our commitment to excellence means that we utilize sustainable practices, ensuring that your landscape remains vibrant and flourishing for years to come.

But it's not all serious business - we infuse every project with a sense of fun and creativity. We believe that landscaping should be an enjoyable journey, and we love to inject elements of surprise and playfulness into our designs. From interactive features to imaginative garden structures, we strive to create outdoor spaces that bring joy and create lasting memories.

Contact us today to embark on an exciting landscaping adventure that will transform your outdoor space into a masterpiece of natural beauty and enjoyment.
